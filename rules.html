Game Guidelines & Rules

Before you start the journey, please read the following guidelines and rules:

  • Respect the in-game environment as you would in real life. Conservation is key.
  • Collaborate with other players in community missions to maximize impact.
  • Pay attention to the lessons taught in each level; they will help you in the real world.
  • Use the AR features to better understand how your actions affect the environment.
  • Complete levels to unlock new content and further your learning.
  • Remember, the goal is not just to win but to learn and apply that knowledge.
