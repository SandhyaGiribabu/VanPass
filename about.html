About Our Game

Discover the journey of water conservation with VanPass Droplet in our interactive game.

Our Mission

Join VanPass Droplet on an adventure to save his village from drought by learning the importance of groundwater conservation. Each level teaches players about the water cycle, groundwater replenishment, and sustainable farming practices.

Engage in challenges that simulate real-world scenarios, helping players apply their knowledge in practical ways. By the end of the game, players will not only have had fun but also gained valuable insights into water conservation and many exciting badges with certification for each chapter.

Game Features

  • Interactive Learning: The game will offer players a series of challenges and scenarios that require strategic decision-making to manage groundwater resources effectively.
  • Point System:Players will earn points or coins based on the choices they make, incentivizing them to learn and apply good practices.
  • Scenarios: The game will simulate real-world conditions such as droughts, excessive rainfall, and water contamination, allowing players to experience the impact of their decisions.
  • Leaderboard: The scores of registered players will be stored online, enabling the identification and certification of "water-smart" youths.
  • Educational Value: The game can be used as a training tool to raise awareness about groundwater management and conservation practices.
  • Quiz and puzzles: The game will have various quiz and puzzles to make the game more interactive and educational.
  • Enhancing accessibility for people with disabilities: Implementing voice recognition features
